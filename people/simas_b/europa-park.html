Europa-Park


23rd July 2025


Europa-Park is a theme park in Rust, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. It's compiled of many areas each representing a different European country, sadly not every European country has a section. Best theme park I've ever been to, amazing rollercoasters and attractions all in all. My favourite rollercoaster has to be Voltron in the Croatian sector designed around Nikola Tesla, Voltron won the "Best rollercoaster" award in 2024, the same year it was opened.
