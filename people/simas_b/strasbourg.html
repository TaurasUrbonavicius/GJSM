Strasbourg


5th August 2025


Strasbourg is the capital city of the Grand Est region, formerly Alsace, in northeastern France. Spent only 1 day here walking around. Visited the cathedral, a wine cellar with wine from 1472, European Parlament, Voodoo museum and more. Beautiful city but a ton of tourists, didn't get to explore absolutely everything in a single day but nonetheless it was amazing.
