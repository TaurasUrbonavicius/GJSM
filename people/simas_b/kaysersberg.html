Kaysersberg


22nd July 2025 and 6th August 2025 + extra


Kaysersberg is a historical town and former commune in Alsace in northeastern France. The name is German for Emperor's Mountain. First town I properly visited in France, lived here with my cousin, aunt and her husband. In total I spent only around a week here. Beautiful town both in nature and architecture, this was my third time here and it's only getting better and better.
