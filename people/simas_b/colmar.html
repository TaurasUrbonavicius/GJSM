Colmar


4th and 5th August 2025


Colmar is a town in the Grand Est region of northeastern France, near the border with Germany. I spent one full day here and walked through it again on the next. The creator of the "Statue Of Liberty" was born here. Beautiful town with truly good scenery and nice people.
