Atlantic Coast


24th July 2025 to 3rd August 2025


Atlantic Coast is a generalisation of the places I visited around the Atlantic Ocean. Main place I stayed at was Saint-Hilaire-la-Forêt where I slept, ate and spent the majority of my time. Saint-Vincent-sur-Jard was my second most visited city, sitting right on the coast of the atlantic ocean it's got great beaches. Other places like the flower park will be shown in pictures.
